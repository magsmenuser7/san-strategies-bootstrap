About

San's
Strategic Story

Sandeep, with 8 years of research in brand strategy, founded Magsmen 4 years ago, a firm known for developing strategies for brands. Recognized for building industry-leading brands, his leadership has brought Magsmen notable success and awards. He's also a registered advocate and a BNI Director Consultant, using his expertise to help startups through his initiative, Launchpad. His client list includes major IPL sponsors and Fortune 25 companies, demonstrating his broad impact. Sandeep's work is more than just business; it's about inspiring others with his vision and commitment to growth across various sectors. He also serves as a personal branding coach and speaker, delivering inspiring sessions that help corporations, students, and individuals build thought-provoking career skills. Through his coaching and speaking engagements, Sandeep motivates and empowers individuals to develop their personal brands and achieve success in their professional endeavors.
